About me

Hi, I’m Joanne Chan, a Swansea University graduate with a strong passion for UX design and front-end development. I enjoy transforming complex problems into simple, engaging digital experiences that put users first. My work blends creativity with technical skills, allowing me to design and build interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and functional. I thrive on collaboration, continuous learning, and turning ideas into meaningful products that enhance everyday interactions. Outside of design, I love exploring new places, discovering creative inspiration, and staying curious about the world around me.
