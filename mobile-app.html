Quiz app (mobile app)

Overview

The project aims to create a quiz app that allows users to customize the question features, which include category, number, and type of questions that they want to answer. This flexibility idea enhances user engagement. Additionally, users can check their history records to track their progress over time. Besides that, the quiz questions are designed from various topics found in an encyclopaedia. Comparing with traditional reading methods, the daily quizzes offer a more engaging and interactive method to learn knowledge. Adopting the short quiz strategy, users can learn new epistemology in a fun and enjoyable manner. The process of gaining knowledge becomes more interesting and memorable without pressures. Hence, this project seeks to develop a concise and user-friendly daily quiz mobile app to encourage learning.

Target group

The daily quiz app belongs to educational games, which are suitable for students and office workers. For persons that are in the stage of studying, the interesting method of learning new knowledge makes them enjoy the process of learning. Moreover, the app is an entertaining approach to satisfy their curiosity and expand their knowledge database. Besides, office workers are also the target groups. The mobile app can provide a fun and engaging way to relieve stress during long hours at work. Additionally, the workers can enjoy the quiz game app during commutes, lunch breaks, or downtime.

Objectives

1) Enhancing knowledge through quizzes:
Provide users with an engaging quiz platform to learn new knowledges across diverse topics. For instance, science, history, sports.

2) Customizable quiz function:
Allow users to personalize the quiz content by selecting question categories, number of questions, and question types. For example, multiple choice and true or false questions.

3) Track and improve performance:
Enable users to monitor their quiz history and highest scores, encouraging them to track progress and motivating them to improve.

4) Easy account management:
Facilitate account-based features, such as saving preferences and accessing past records, to enhance user convenience and personalization.

5) Increase engagement with notifications:
Send daily reminders to encourage consistent engagement and make learning a regular habit through notifications about new quizzes.

6)Accessible interface design:
Ensure a clean, user-friendly interface that allows users of all ages navigate and enjoy the app smoothly

Overview of the user journey

The app’s user is required to begin with an account creation. Users need to input basic information such as their email, password, and name. They need to retype the password to confirm the correctness while fulfilling the account applications. After registering, users can log in and access the home page. This page is the main navigation area of this app. The home page has two main options and a toolbar. The "START QUIZ" button allows users to begin with a new quiz. The "CHECK MY RECORD" button would take them to a page that displays their quiz history and scores. This history page allows users to track their updated best performance and see which areas they might want to improve.

The toolbar provides quick-access icons for easy navigating. This toolbar has a "Home" icon that allows to return to the main screen. Also, the "Log out" symbol can be used to log out of the app directly. The toolbar is available on all the interfaces, except the login and sign-up pages. It provides a consistent navigation of managing the app. When users press the "START QUIZ," they can customize their quiz by selecting topics, the numbers of questions and question types. This personalisation function enhances the quiz experience and engaging.

After completing a quiz, users can receive their scores, which is saved in their history pages and the app database. Pressing the "CHECK MY RECORD" button, users can review their scores and track their progress to see their highest marks. This simple and concise design makes is user-friendly to create an account, take quizzes, and monitor their improvement.
