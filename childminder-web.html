What is childminder?

  • Cares for children in their own home for 3+ hours/day
  • Must register if caring for under-8s for 2+ paid hours/day
  • Gets a Unique Reference Number (URN) for Ofsted checks
  • Must meet health and safety standards or face penalties
  • Offers flexible care: part-time, full-time, or after-school
